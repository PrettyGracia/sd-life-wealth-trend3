Essential Guide to General Insurance

What is General Insurance?

General insurance is a safeguard against financial losses arising from unexpected events. It includes a range of policies such as health, property, motor, and travel insurance to protect individuals and businesses.

Common Types of General Insurance

  • Motor Insurance: Covers damages to vehicles and third-party liabilities.
  • Property Insurance: Protects buildings, homes, and commercial establishments.
  • Liability Insurance: Shields businesses and individuals from legal claims.
  • Health Insurance: Covers medical expenses and hospitalization costs.

How General Insurance Helps You

Insurance provides financial stability by reducing risks associated with accidents, theft, and natural calamities. By investing in a reliable insurance policy, you can secure your assets and focus on long-term growth.
